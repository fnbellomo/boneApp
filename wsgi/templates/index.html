Bone App.

The Idea

Bone App is a basic structure to avoid having to create from scratch your new web scientific application, which works with Bootstrap to a unique design for any device, Flask and Python to work from the server, a very easy way to validate certificates of Aouth2 to link your apliacacion with social networks and JS for amazing graphics.
